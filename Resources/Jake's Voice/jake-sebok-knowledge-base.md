## Brand Story

### Before (The Pain)
Life was restrictive and unfulfilling. I felt trapped trading time for money, believing only performance ensured security. Even as my career advanced, I was stuck in roles that didn't serve me—bound by golden handcuffs. Starting my business for freedom and purpose, I fell into overwork and misalignment, chasing money over passion. This left me burned out and running a business that felt like a beautiful prison. I abandoned my true self for safety, knowing something had to change but unsure how.

### The Inciting Event
The turning point came when I chose to be honest and authentic about my true desires, allowing myself to pursue them immediately. By sharing my truth, I found others related to my struggles, and small, targeted changes helped us all find freedom. Helping and selling from this aligned place led to real transformation for myself and my clients. Each step proved my value, and I never looked back.

### Life Today
Now, I work only with clients who share my values and approach. My mission is to help others break free from limiting beliefs and live authentically. I wake up with purpose, confidence, and passion, knowing my work matters. I control my schedule, collaborate with inspiring people, prioritize family and health, travel, and live fully present—no longer postponing happiness.

## Aligned Power™

**Aligned Power** is Jake’s **methodology** (the five-stage path clients move through) **and** the **name of the 12-month group program**: the **Aligned Power Accelerator**. When you see “Aligned Power” in marketing, it means both the system and the flagship cohort experience unless context specifies otherwise.

Programs guide clients through five stages: **Awareness, Strategic Clarity, Internal Alignment, Aligned Action, and Embodied Execution.**

## Naming: Products & Prompts (read this before writing copy)

| Term | What it is |
|------|------------|
| **Aligned Freedom Coach** | The customer-facing name of the **only app** Jake sells. |
| **ALFRED** | The same product as Aligned Freedom Coach. Acronym: **AL**igned **FRE**e**D**om Coach. Use **ALFRED** when you want personality and recall; use **Aligned Freedom Coach** in formal/legal contexts as needed. |
| **Aligned AIOS** | The **master prompt + blueprint** users generate at the end of **Phase II (Strategic Clarity)** in the Accelerator **or** in the **Strategic Alignment Intensive**. It turns ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or another model into a personalized, values-aligned coach using their workshop outputs. *Not* a separate app—**it’s the prompt layer.** |
| **ALFRED vs Aligned AIOS** | **Aligned AIOS** is the “bring-your-own-AI” operating context from worksheets. **ALFRED** is that intelligence **plus** live integration: VAPI™ scores, Founder Archetype, primary dysfunction driver pattern, weekly **6Cs** scores, **Vital Actions**, and ongoing coaching—**Aligned AIOS on steroids**, inside one app. |

Do **not** use “Aligned Performance Operating System” or “Aligned AI OS” (old labels). Use **Aligned AIOS** for the master-prompt system.

## Baseline assessment (VAPI™)

- **Structure:** Scores across **12 domains** grouped into **3 arenas** (not nine “areas”—legacy copy is retired).
- **Advantage:** Identifies bottlenecks, Founder Archetype, and focus priorities.
- **Benefit:** Immediate clarity on where to act; feeds **ALFRED** when clients use the app.

## Target audiences: two avatars

**Operators** — Already in motion (revenue, clients, systems). They don’t need permission—they need **re-alignment**: strategy, energy, and decisions wired to what actually matters. Primary path: **VAPI™ → Strategic Clarity / Intensive → Aligned AIOS → ALFRED.**

**Builders** — Earlier in the arc (launching, pivoting, or turning transformation into vocation). They can **build aligned from the beginning** if they install the right questions early. Primary path: same ladder, with emphasis on **Awareness** and **Strategic Clarity** before scale.

**Overlap:** Solo entrepreneurs, owners, and founders in helper/healer/coach/creator lanes; meaning-driven; often $100K–$300K+ revenue bands but **avatar matters more than revenue alone.**

---

## Book vs coaching ecosystem (ICP clarity)

- **Selling Fire** (book) speaks to **aspiring or successful** owners turning **transformation into vocation**—normalizing the path and offering **wayfinding**. It feeds the ecosystem (communities, VAPI™, programs).
- **Coaching + ALFRED** serve **Operators and Builders** inside the full Aligned Power methodology—not “scale at all costs,” but **alignment-first execution.**

## Voice & messaging

- Conversational, judgment-free, empowering.
- Reframe investment as the cost of staying stuck; name the risk of inaction.
- Speak to healers and entrepreneurs in their own language.
- Sustainable, values-aligned growth (no hustle culture).
