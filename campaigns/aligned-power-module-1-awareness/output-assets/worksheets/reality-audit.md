# The Reality Audit

**Aligned Power — Module 1: Awareness**

> You've completed the VAPI™ Assessment. *(If not, take it before continuing—your results are the foundation for this worksheet.)*
>
> The VAPI gave you the quantitative picture: numbers, scores, tiers. Now we need the qualitative picture: the story underneath the scores. This is the most important pre-work of this entire program. It surfaces what's actually going on beneath the surface of your life and business.

---

## How to Use This Worksheet

- **Time:** Set aside 60–90 minutes of uninterrupted time. Not between meetings. Not while multitasking.
- **Space:** Find a quiet place. Close everything else. Give yourself permission to be brutally honest.
- **Rules:** There are no right answers. There are no impressive answers. There are only honest ones.
- **Tip:** Don't rush to solutions. In this stage, depth beats speed.

*Pro tip:* Speaking your answers out loud (e.g. [Wispr Flow](https://ref.wisprflow.ai/jacob-sebok) or similar) often surfaces the truest reflection—especially for the deeper questions.
