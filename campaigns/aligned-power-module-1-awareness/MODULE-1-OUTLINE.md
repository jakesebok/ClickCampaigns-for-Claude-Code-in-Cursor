# Module 1: Awareness — Aligned Power Accelerator

**Purpose:** Introduce the underlying theory of Aligned Power, complete the Reality Audit, and bridge participants from Phase I (Awareness) into Phase II (Strategic Clarity). This module runs *before* the VAPI in some flows, or *after* the VAPI as the first substantive content—depending on program design.

**Positioning:** This is not mythological. We're not helping them "find their authentic self." We're helping them operate in alignment with the real person that hides below the performance—because 95% of behavior is driven by subconscious impulses, and being out of rapport with the subconscious is the problem (Erickson). The hero complex ("if 100k feels like this, 300k must feel 3x harder") and other limiting beliefs keep their future a prison instead of a haven. Learning to delegate—and aligning their business with their nervous system—is the path.

---

## Module Goal

**Outcome for the learner:** "I understand why alignment matters at a subconscious level, I've named the limiting beliefs (including hero complex) that keep my future a prison instead of a haven, and I'm ready for Strategic Clarity to excavate who I really am—not who I think I'm supposed to be."

## Key Concepts (Video 1.0)

- **The core question** — "In what ways would achieving your current definition of success threaten your happiness, your health, your relationships, or your sense of freedom?" / "How are the ways you're currently working to attain your goals keeping you from ever truly achieving them?"
- **95% subconscious** — The "little scientist" (Einstein hair, toddler, Inside Out) runs the control center; keeps you safe; sabotages when it sees danger
- **Hero complex / hyper-self-reliance** — Limiting belief: "If 100k feels like this, 300k must feel 3x harder." Can't delegate. Future becomes a prison instead of a haven. Learning to delegate is non-negotiable.
- **Haven vs. prison** — Achieving the wrong goal = prison. Aligning with the real you = haven. Your future becoming a haven depends on it.
- **Larry story** — Conflict of interests: wants more clients, knows what to do, sabotages (TikTok, nap, wall). Why? Serving wrong people. Success would've been a prison. Subconscious protected him.
- **Erickson** — "Being out of rapport with the subconscious" is the core problem. Rapport = you move. Out of rapport = you spin.
- **Excavation, not mythology** — We're not finding an "authentic self"; we're aligning actions with who they *are* vs. who they think they're *supposed* to be
- **Strategic Clarity = excavation** — The Alignment Arc framework exists to surface the real person, dismantle limiting beliefs, and align actions—including capacity to delegate

## Bridge to Phase II

Phase II (Strategic Clarity / The Alignment Arc) is where we:
- Excavate the real person through Real Reasons, Driving Fire, Values, The Future You
- Turn that into a revenue number (The Revenue Bridge)
- Build a Vital Action Plan
- Install it all into Aligned Freedom Coach

Module 1 sets the *why*: we're not fixing a broken person. We're getting back in rapport with the one that's been running the show all along—and aligning our actions with them.
