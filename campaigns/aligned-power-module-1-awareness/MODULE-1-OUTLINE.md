# Module 1: Awareness — Aligned Power Accelerator

**Purpose:** Introduce the underlying theory of Aligned Power, complete the Reality Audit, and bridge participants from Phase I (Awareness) into Phase II (Strategic Clarity). This module runs *before* the VAPI in some flows, or *after* the VAPI as the first substantive content—depending on program design.

**Positioning:** This is not mythological. We're not helping them "find their authentic self." We're helping them operate in alignment with the real person that hides below the performance—because 95% of behavior is driven by subconscious impulses, and being out of rapport with the subconscious is the problem (Erickson).

---

## Module Goal

**Outcome for the learner:** "I understand why alignment matters at a subconscious level, I've named what's actually going on beneath the surface, and I'm ready for Strategic Clarity to excavate who I really am—not who I think I'm supposed to be."

---

## Key Concepts (Video 1.0)

- **95% subconscious** — Most behavior is driven by impulses below awareness
- **Erickson** — "Being out of rapport with the subconscious" is the core problem
- **Excavation, not mythology** — We're not finding an "authentic self"; we're aligning actions with who they *are* vs. who they think they're *supposed* to be
- **Strategic Clarity = excavation** — The Alignment Arc framework exists to surface the real person and align actions with that
- **Performance vs. reality** — The real person hides below the performance; the goal is rapport, not transcendence

---

## Bridge to Phase II

Phase II (Strategic Clarity / The Alignment Arc) is where we:
- Excavate the real person through Real Reasons, Driving Fire, Values, The Future You
- Turn that into a revenue number (The Revenue Bridge)
- Build a Vital Action Plan
- Install it all into VAPOS

Module 1 sets the *why*: we're not fixing a broken person. We're getting back in rapport with the one that's been running the show all along—and aligning our actions with them.
