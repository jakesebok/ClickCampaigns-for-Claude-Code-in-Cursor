# Homepage — Full Page Review

**Reviewers:** Ryan (Copy), Cassidy (Design), Dylan (SEO), Alexis (Funnel)  
**Date:** March 2025  
**Goal:** Optimize for high conversion

---

## Page Flow Assessment

**Current:** Hero → Trusted By → Empathy → The Work → Testimonials → CTA

**Verdict:** Flow is correct. Hook → Empathy → Solution → Proof → CTA. No structural changes needed.

---

## Section-by-Section

### Hero
- **H1:** Strong. "Stop building someone else's success." — Provocative, clear.
- **Subhead:** "You don't have a discipline problem. You have an alignment problem." — Excellent reframe. Ties to The Loop card.
- **CTAs:** Primary (Take the VAPI™) + Secondary (See How It Works). Good hierarchy.
- **Quote:** "Beautiful prison" — On-brand. Good.
- **Fix:** Remove outdated "Slate & Spark" from code comment.

### Trusted By
- **Verdict:** Good. Social proof. No changes.

### Empathy Section
- **Verdict:** Recently optimized. Strong. No changes.

### Bridge: Empathy → The Work
- **Gap:** The page jumps from pain (empathy cards) to solution (The Work) without a transition. A one-line bridge would soften the turn and increase receptivity.
- **Recommendation:** Add a bridge line before "The Work" headline: "It doesn't have to stay this way." or "There's a different way to build."

### The Work
- **Headline:** "I don't optimize for output. I optimize for alignment." — Strong differentiator.
- **Recommendation:** Add a subhead that names the three pillars and the payoff: "Awareness. Internal Alignment. Embodied Execution. The path from the cage to the life you actually want."
- **Cards:** Copy is solid. Awareness ties to "gap." Internal Alignment ties to "self-sabotage." Embodied Execution ties to "sustainable." No changes.

### Testimonials
- **Headline:** "Real transformation. Real results." — Good.
- **Verdict:** Carousel works. No changes.

### CTA
- **Headline:** "Ready to stop building the cage?" — Ties to hero quote. Good.
- **Body:** "Start with the free VAPI™ Assessment. 72 questions. ~12 minutes." — Good.
- **Gap:** Target audience fear = "investing and ending up more depleted." The CTA doesn't explicitly address "free, no commitment, no payment." Adding that reduces friction.
- **Recommendation:** Add "Free. No payment required." or "No commitment. No payment. Just clarity." to reinforce the low-risk entry.
- **Secondary CTA:** "See All Offerings" — Fine. Goes to work-with-me.
