# Copy Critique: Home Page

## Changes Applied

### Headlines
- **Hero support copy:** Tightened the hero paragraph to frame low motivation as a business-life fit problem, not a discipline problem.

### CTAs
- **Hero secondary CTA:** Changed "See How It Works" to "See Your Next Step" for a clearer next-click promise.
- **How to Get Started intro:** Reframed the section to "Get your map" and "choose the level of support that fits."
- **Step 3 / lower-page CTA language:** Replaced vague "Take Action" / "See All Offerings" phrasing with more specific next-step language.

### Copy Improvements
- **Reframe section:** Shifted from a softer "might be growing" construction to a more direct explanation of how misaligned growth creates internal resistance.
- **Support-path section:** Clarified that the goal is choosing the right level of support instead of adding random complexity.

## Notes for Review
- I kept the core home-page structure and offer flow intact. This pass focused on persuasion clarity and CTA specificity, not page architecture.
