## Brand Story

### Before (The Pain)
Life was restrictive and unfulfilling. I felt trapped trading time for money, believing only performance ensured security. Even as my career advanced, I was stuck in roles that didn’t serve me—bound by golden handcuffs. Starting my business for freedom and purpose, I fell into overwork and misalignment, chasing money over passion. This left me burned out and running a business that felt like a beautiful prison. I abandoned my true self for safety, knowing something had to change but unsure how.

### The Inciting Event
The turning point came when I chose to be honest and authentic about my true desires, allowing myself to pursue them immediately. By sharing my truth, I found others related to my struggles, and small, targeted changes helped us all find freedom. Helping and selling from this aligned place led to real transformation for myself and my clients. Each step proved my value, and I never looked back.

### Life Today
Now, I work only with clients who share my values and approach. My mission is to help others break free from limiting beliefs and live authentically. I wake up with purpose, confidence, and passion, knowing my work matters. I control my schedule, collaborate with inspiring people, prioritize family and health, travel, and live fully present—no longer postponing happiness.

---

## Primary Brand Goal

The goal is for Aligned Power to help entrepreneurs who feel trapped by their businesses rediscover their vision and break free from burnout. I want them to build sustainable, values-driven growth so they feel energized and inspired by their work and life. For those just starting, I help them ask the right questions from the beginning to build aligned, fulfilling businesses. The brand serves as a filter and beacon for those seeking meaningful, authentic success.

## Target Audience

**Who:** Solo entrepreneurs, business owners, and founders earning $100,000–$300,000 annually, usually with teams under three. Healers, coaches, content creators (massage therapists, reiki healers, chiropractors, PTs, counselors, transformation coaches).

**Demographics:** Age 28–50 (peak 32–45), U.S./Canada/UK/Australia, $80K–$250K household, business revenue $150K–$750K/year, often partnered with 1–3 kids.

**Psychographics:** High standards, meaning-driven, value freedom and integrity, tend to collect strategies but struggle to commit to one plan, don’t trust intuition under pressure.

**Pain points:** Too many priorities, lack of direction, inconsistent follow-through, burnout, compromising values for money, business affecting home life, outgrown old success but lack new vision. They need a clear compass, simple math, and a system to protect what matters under pressure.
