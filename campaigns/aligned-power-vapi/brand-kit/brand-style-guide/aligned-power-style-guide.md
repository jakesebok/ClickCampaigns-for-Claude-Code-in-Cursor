# Aligned Power Brand Style Guide

*Used for VAPI Assessment campaign and all Aligned Power assets.*

---

## 1. VISUAL IDENTITY

### Colors
- **Primary:** #582233 (main actions, buttons, highlights)
- **Secondary:** #334750 (secondary actions, accents)
- **Accent:** #D4AA70 (calls to action, emphasis)
- **Background:** #F4ECE3
- **Text:** #582233
- **Muted:** #C6B8A7
- **Border:** #C6B8A7

### Typography
- **Headings:** Cormorant Garamond, weight 700
- **Body:** Source Sans Pro, weight 400 (Regular), 600 (Bold)

### Spacing & Layout
- **Base unit:** 4px
- **Border radius:** 0.5rem
