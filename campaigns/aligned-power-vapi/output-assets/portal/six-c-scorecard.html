How to create a powerful Vital Action

Your Vital Action is the single aligned move that, if you protect it, makes everything else easier or unnecessary. Gary Keller’s work on focus and cascade helped shape this idea—we call it your Vital Action.

Ask yourself: What’s my Vital Action—the single aligned move that, by doing it, makes everything else easier or unnecessary?

  • Go small — narrow down to the single most important action or shift.
  • Be specific — “Protect my morning block” beats “work on focus.”
  • Make it actionable — something you can actually do this week.

One Vital Action knocks over the next. Start with the one that matters most.
