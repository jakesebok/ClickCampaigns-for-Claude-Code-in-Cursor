### Step 2: For each person, list 5 qualities you admire

These should be **observable behaviors**, not vague compliments.

**Angles to consider:**
- How they act under pressure (calm, decisive, resilient, courageous)
- How they treat people when they have power (fairness, respect, candor, generosity)
- What standards they refuse to negotiate (integrity, excellence, responsibility)
- How they set boundaries (saying no, protecting family, protecting health)
- How they handle conflict (direct, kind, honest, repair-focused)
- How they relate to work (craftsmanship, focus, consistency, long-term thinking)
- How they relate to themselves (self-trust, discipline, humility, emotional regulation)

**Be specific:**
- Vague: "smart" → Specific: "explains complex things simply without making people feel small"
- Vague: "nice" → Specific: "tells the truth with care, even when it's uncomfortable"
- Vague: "hard-working" → Specific: "finishes what they start and protects deep work blocks"
