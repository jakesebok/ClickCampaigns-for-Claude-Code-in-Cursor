## Voice & Tone
Read before every drafting session: `Resources/Jake's Voice/JAKE-SEBOK-WRITING-STYLE.md`

Key voice notes:
- Warm and personal — Jake builds a bond before he teaches
- Spiritual in nature, rational in language (not woo-woo, not corporate)
- Poetic rhythm: short punchy sentences interrupted by longer, sweeping ones
- The "Jake pause": a beat before the important line
- Uses: "Here's the thing.", "And that's the problem.", "So let's talk about that."
- Starts chapters with a scene or a story — never a definition
- Ends sections with a question or a reframe that lingers
