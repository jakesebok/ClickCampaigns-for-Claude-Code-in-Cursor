# Hero Copy Critique — Jake Sebok Homepage

**Reviewers:** Ryan (Direct Response Copywriter), Dylan (SEO Expert)  
**Date:** March 2025  
**Asset:** Homepage hero section

---

## Ryan — Copy Review

### Eyebrow (current)
> Slate & Spark · Jake Sebok

**Issue:** "Slate & Spark" is an internal brand concept, not a value proposition. It doesn't tell visitors what they're getting or why they should care. Eyebrows should prime the reader and support the headline.

**Recommendation:** Replace with something that sets context and reinforces the offer:
- **Option A (recommended):** `Values-Aligned Performance · Jake Sebok` — Direct, brand-aligned, introduces the core concept.
- **Option B:** `Alignment Over Output · Jake Sebok` — Punchy, differentiator-forward.
- **Option C:** `Performance Coaching for Entrepreneurs · Jake Sebok` — Clear, category-defining.

### Headline (current)
> Stop building someone else's success.

**Verdict:** Strong. Provocative. Creates immediate tension. The orange highlight on "someone else's" works. No change needed.

### Subhead (current)
> You don't have a discipline problem. You have an alignment problem. When what you're building finally matches who you are—execution stops being a war with yourself.

**Verdict:** Excellent. Reframes the pain (discipline → alignment), offers the payoff. "Execution stops being a war with yourself" is a strong close. No change needed.

### Quote (current)
> "Your business shouldn't be a beautiful prison. It should be the best expression of who you actually are."

**Verdict:** On-brand, memorable. The "beautiful prison" metaphor echoes the knowledge base. Keep.

---

## Dylan — SEO Review

### Eyebrow
**Current:** "Slate & Spark" — Zero search intent. Not a keyword.

**Recommendation:** Use a phrase that supports primary keywords without stuffing:
- `Values-Aligned Performance` — Aligns with "values-aligned performance coaching" (primary keyword).
- `Performance Coaching for Entrepreneurs` — Captures "performance coaching" + "entrepreneurs" (target audience).

### H1
**Current:** "Stop building someone else's success." — Punchy but no explicit keywords.

**Recommendation:** Keep the H1 as-is for conversion. The page title and meta description already carry "Values-Aligned Performance Coaching for Entrepreneurs." The H1's job is to hook, not to rank. Avoid keyword-stuffing the hero headline.

### Meta / Page Title
**Current:** "Jake Sebok — Values-Aligned Performance Coaching for Entrepreneurs" — Good. Primary keyword + name + audience. No change.
