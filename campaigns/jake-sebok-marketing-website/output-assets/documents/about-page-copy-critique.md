# About Page — Copy Critique & Rewrite

**Reviewer:** Ryan (Direct Response Copywriter)  
**Date:** March 2025

---

## Current Issues

### Headlines
- **"Credentials"** — Generic. Doesn't sell.
- **"I've walked the path. I'm not an observer—I'm a survivor and thriver on it."** — The sentiment is good but it's buried as a subhead. Could be stronger as a headline.
- **"Certifications"** / **"Experience"** — Bullet labels, not headlines. No emotional pull.
- **"What Clients Say"** / **"Real transformation. Real results."** — Same as homepage. Feels recycled.

### Authority Gap
- Experience section is one short paragraph. "Leadership roles across multiple industries. Scaled businesses to multi-million dollar revenue." — Vague. No specifics.
- Missing: intellectual lineage (Thurman, Campbell, Jung, Brown), what makes the approach different, proof of methodology, why he's qualified to coach *this* audience.
- Certifications are a list. No context for why they matter (ICF = gold standard; MCPC = top tier; NLP = addresses root cause).

---

## Recommendations

### 1. Credentials Section — Stronger Headlines + More Content

**New headline:** "I'm not an observer. I'm a survivor and thriver on this path."

**Certifications card — new headline:** "Trained at the highest level."  
Add context: ICF-accredited, Master Certified (top tier), NLP for subconscious work.

**Experience card — new headline:** "Proven in the arena."  
Expand with specifics: 20x revenue in 9 months, multi-million dollar businesses, leadership across industries, always people over metrics.

### 2. Add "How I Work" Section

Headline: "I coach the whole human being—not just the business."

Body: Where competitors optimize for output, I optimize for alignment. I use NLP-based parts work to address the root cause of self-sabotage. I build Alignment Blueprints from your actual values. Milestone-based pricing shifts risk to me, not you. This isn't peak performance. It's sustainable performance—because peaks imply valleys, and valleys carry a cost you were never willing to pay.

### 3. Add "Foundations" Section

Headline: "Built on thinkers who understood the human journey."

Body: Howard Thurman. Joseph Campbell's Hero's Journey. Carl Jung's psychology. Brené Brown's belonging work. I don't just coach tactics. I help you let go of old identities and return to the fire that was always yours.

### 4. Testimonials Section

**New headline:** "What clients say when the work is done."  
Or: "Transformation isn't theoretical. Here's proof."

Keep "Real transformation. Real results." as subhead if desired, or replace with something About-page specific.
