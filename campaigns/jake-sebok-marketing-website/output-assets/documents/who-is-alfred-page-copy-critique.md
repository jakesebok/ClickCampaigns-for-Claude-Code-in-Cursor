# Copy Critique: Who Is ALFRED Page

## Changes Applied

### Headlines
- **Hero headline and support copy:** Shifted the opening away from ecosystem-specific nouns and toward the immediate promise: clarity in your pocket when pressure hits.

### CTAs
- **Primary CTAs:** Standardized the page around "Start My 7-Day Trial" so the action is clear for a new visitor.

### Copy Improvements
- **Avatar fit:** Rewrote the major section intros around a high-performing executive / founder use case: real decisions, noisy weeks, too much context in their own head.
- **Jargon reduction:** Replaced early references to Blueprint, VAPI™, 6Cs, and similar ecosystem language with plain-English benefits like priorities, constraints, context, and next best move.
- **Comparison table:** Tightened the ALFRED side so it explains why the product is useful under pressure instead of assuming the reader already understands the internal framework.
- **Audience section:** Reframed "who this is for" around executives and founders who need clear thinking in the moment, not only insiders already bought into the ecosystem.
- **Founder quote block:** Tightened the quote so it lands on continuity and decision support rather than abstract "docs in a drawer" language.

## Notes for Review
- The biggest issue on the prior version was not weak writing quality. It was translation. The page understood the ecosystem better than a cold but qualified prospect would. This pass fixes that by landing the use case first and the framework second.
